Welcome to the Iowa Gambling Task

Your goal is to maximize the government budget while balancing economic growth, labor capacity, crime, and pollution. Each deck represents a policy decision. Choose wisely, as each decision has consequences.

  • Deck 1: Increases economic growth but increases pollution.
  • Deck 2: Increases labor capacity but increases crime.
  • Deck 3: Decreases pollution but reduces economic growth.
  • Deck 4: Decreases crime but reduces labor capacity.

You start with $2000. Good luck!
