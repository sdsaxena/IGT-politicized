Welcome to the Game!

In this game, you'll make decisions that affect various factors such as economic growth, crime rates, and pollution levels. Your goal is to balance these factors while managing a budget.

Instructions: In this task, you play a "gambling" game. You need to choose one of the 4 buttons (A, B, C, or D) with you mouse. Each time, you have the opportunity to win some money, but you may also sometimes have to pay a fee to the government. After each trial, you need to collect your money, which will adjust your total funds. You start with a loan of $2000. There are 100 trials (~5 minutes in total). Go on until the game stops and see how much you can make on top of the loan of $2000. Press the space bar to start. Good luck!
